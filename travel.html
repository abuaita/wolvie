Travel and Accommodations

How to Get to Aurora

There are several ways of getting to Aurora. If you're in the NYC area, we'll be arranging a bus that will leave Friday mid-morning from NYC and leave Sunday from the venue. We'll ask for headcount later!

You can also drive yourself, the venue has free parking. Aurora, NY is about a 4.5 hour drive from NYC.

If you're coming from outside of New York, there are airports in Syracuse (60 min to venue), Rochester (80 min to venue), and Ithaca (30 min to venue). If you're flying into an airport, we can connect you with others to share a rideshare or cab.

If you are in the Northeast, there are also a number of buses that go to Ithaca.

Accommodations

We have reserved rooms for everyone in Aurora within walking distance (<5 min), so no need to book anything right now. We'll update here with booking instructions by early March. Please let us know if you have any special requirements or questions about accommodations!

Because we are saving rooms, if you know you cannot come, please let us know on the RSVP page.
