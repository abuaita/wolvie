Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our weekend

When is the wedding?

Our wedding will take place on Saturday, September 26, 2026, at the Inns of Aurora in Aurora, NY. We'll throw a welcome party on Friday, so please save the whole weekend to join us! More details will be on the Schedule page as we finalize them.

What should I wear?

For Friday, dress code is festive/cocktail attire. For Saturday, we ask that you wear black-tie please. (We'll be in tuxedos, but if you don't have one, please wear a dark suit.)

Where should I stay?

We have reserved rooms for everyone in Aurora, including 5 of the Inns and several rental properties. We'll update here how to book by early March.

How do I get there?

There are several ways of getting to Aurora. First, if you're in the NYC area, we'll be arranging a [party] bus that will leave Friday mid-morning and return Sunday around 12pm. (We'll email and ask for headcount later!) You can also drive yourself, the venue has free parking.



If you're coming from outside of New York, there are airports in Syracuse (60 min to venue), Rochester (80 min to venue), and Ithaca (30 min to venue). If you're flying into an airport, we can connect you with others to share a rideshare or take a cab!



If you are in the Northeast, there are also a number of buses that go to Ithaca!

Are children welcome?

The venue is an adults-only property, but we can arrange for childcare onsite for young children during the ceremony and reception. Please let us know if you need this!

Will the ceremony be indoors or outdoors?

Both the welcome party and the wedding will be outdoors by the lake! We have some backup options in case of inclement weather.

Can I bring a plus one?

Please refer to your invitation for details about plus ones. If you have any questions, feel free to reach out to us directly.

How do I RSVP?

RSVP information will be included with your formal invitation. We kindly ask that you respond by the date indicated.

What about dietary restrictions?

Please let us know about any dietary restrictions or allergies when you RSVP.
